Admission Requirements(College)

Requirements for Incoming First Year:

  1. Form 138 (High School Rating Card)
  2. Alternative Learning Center (ALS) Rating for ALS Graduate.
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character (from the Guidance Counselor of the school last attended)
  4. Copy of Birth Certificate from National Statistics Office (NSO)
  5. Three (3) copies 2" x 2" and 1 copy 1" x 1" colored pictures with white background

Requirements for Transferees:

  1. Certified True Copy of Grades (from the institution last attended)
  2. Certificate of Honorable Dismissal
  3. Certificate of Good Moral Character (from the Guidance Counselor of the school last attended)l
